Short Intro of GCT Bhakakr

Established in 2010, Government College of Technology Bhakkar has consistently delivered top academic results. With over 90% of students achieving success in their careers, GCT Bhakkar is recognized for excellence in technical education and its role in shaping bright futures.

Departments

CIT

CIT at GCT is a high-level diploma focusing on programming, web development, networking, and software development. It also builds strong business communication and technical skills for a successful IT career.

ET

The Electrical Diploma at GCT is a rigorous program that trains students in electrical systems, power distribution, and control technologies. Graduates gain hands-on skills and professional knowledge for careers in installation, maintenance, and electrical engineering support.

Eltr

The Electronics Diploma at GCT focuses on modern electronic systems, circuits, and digital technologies. It prepares students with practical skills in troubleshooting, designing, and maintaining advanced electronic devices and systems.

MT

The Mechanical Diploma at GCT provides in-depth training in machine design, manufacturing, and industrial maintenance. Students develop strong technical skills to work effectively in mechanical engineering and production industries.

CV

The Mechanical Diploma at GCT provides in-depth training in machine design, manufacturing, and industrial maintenance. Students develop strong technical skills to work effectively in mechanical engineering and production industries.

Acadmic Courses

IT

Government College of Technology Bhakkar offers a one-year IT Computer course covering Graphic Design, Canva, Photoshop, and Illustrator. It builds creative and technical design skills for professional use.

Shuutring

Government College of Technology Bhakkar offers a six-month Shuttering course focused on practical construction skills. It trains students in formwork techniques used in modern building projects.

Painting

Government College of Technology Bhakkar offers a three-month Painting course that teaches basic and advanced painting techniques. It prepares students for professional work in decorative and industrial painting.

Wring

Government College of Technology Bhakkar offers a three-month Wiring course that provides practical training in electrical installation and maintenance. It helps students develop essential skills for electrical work.

Facilities

Masjid

The college also includes a mosque within its facilities, providing students a peaceful place to offer prayers regularly. It promotes moral values and spiritual well-being alongside education.

Canteen

The college has a canteen that provides fresh food and refreshments for students. It offers a comfortable space for relaxation and social interaction during breaks.

Free Transport

The college provides free transport facilities for students from nearby areas. It ensures safe, convenient, and timely travel to and from the campus.

Grounds

The college has wide playgrounds for various sports and physical activities. They encourage teamwork, fitness, and a healthy campus environment.
